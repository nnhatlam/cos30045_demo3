About Energy Company

We're here to make TV shopping easier! We take the complicated energy information from the government and present it in a way that's simple to understand. No confusing numbers, just clear comparisons to help you pick the right TV.

Think of us as your helpful friend who's done the research for you. We gather official data about TVs and show you what really matters - how much they'll cost to run.

Our Goal

Help you find a TV that's great for your wallet and the environment. We compare different models so you can see which ones use less electricity and save you money in the long run.

Where Our Information Comes From

The Source

We get all our TV information from reg.energyrating.gov.au - that's the Australian government's official website where all TV manufacturers have to register their products' energy ratings.

How We Organize It

We take the raw data and make it easier to read by:

  • ✓ Collecting the latest information from government sources
  • ✓ Checking the numbers to see patterns and trends
  • ✓ Fixing any errors or missing information
  • ✓ Making sure everything is in the same format for easy comparison

Things to Keep in Mind

  • ✓ We don't collect any personal information - everything comes from public government sources
  • ✓ TVs are tested in labs, so your home usage might be a bit different
  • ✓ We only compare TVs of similar sizes - it wouldn't be fair to compare a small TV to a huge one!
  • ✓ TV makers provide the data, and the government checks it regularly

Our Commitment to You

We believe everyone deserves honest, clear information when shopping for a TV. Here's what we promise:

  • ✓ We show you the facts as they are - no exaggerations
  • ✓ We only compare similar-sized TVs so comparisons are fair
  • ✓ We explain how TVs are tested so you know what to expect
  • ✓ We only use public information - your privacy matters
