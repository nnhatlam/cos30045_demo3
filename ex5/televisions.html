What the Numbers Mean

All our information comes from the government's energy rating website, where TV makers have to report how much power their TVs use.

What to Look For

  • Star Rating: More stars = uses less electricity = saves you more money!
  • Power Usage: How much electricity the TV uses when you're watching it
  • Screen Size: Bigger TVs usually use more power - so only compare TVs of similar sizes
  • Display Type: Different types (LED, OLED, QLED) can affect how much power they use

Good to Know

⚠️ These numbers come from testing in labs with specific settings. Your TV at home might use a bit more or less power depending on how bright you set it and which features you use. But these numbers are still great for comparing different models!
